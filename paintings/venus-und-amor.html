    Venus stands in front of a dark background on stony ground. She wears nothing more than a large hat with a feather. In addition she holds a veil in both hands to her left. A slight breeze blows it across her vulva, which the transparency of the veil does not hide. Cupid stands at the left edge of the painting on a small pedestal with the bow in his right hand and looks up at Venus.
